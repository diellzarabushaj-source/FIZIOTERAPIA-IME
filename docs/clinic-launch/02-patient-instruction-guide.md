# Patient Instruction Guide

Status: Draft for clinic use.

## Purpose

This guide explains Fizioterapia ime to a patient in simple language.

The patient uses the platform to:

- open the exercise plan from the physiotherapist
- complete daily exercises
- report pain score 0–10
- use AI Movement Check when available
- see progress
- follow safety instructions

## Important

The patient does not create an account.

The patient does not create a plan.

The patient only uses the code given by the physiotherapist.

## How to enter

You have two options:

### Option 1 — QR code

1. Open the camera on your phone.
2. Scan the QR code given by your physiotherapist.
3. Open the link.
4. Your dashboard opens.

### Option 2 — Code

1. Open:

```text
/patient-portal
```

2. Enter your patient code.
3. Click:

```text
Hyr në dashboard
```

## Your code

Your code is personal.

Do not share it with other people.

One code belongs to one patient only.

Example:

```text
ARB-123456
```

## What you see in the dashboard

You can see:

- your active plan
- day of plan
- exercises
- sets/reps/frequency
- instructions
- completed exercises
- pain score history
- AI score history
- messages from physiotherapist

## How to complete an exercise

1. Read the exercise instructions.
2. Do the exercise slowly and carefully.
3. Stop if pain becomes strong.
4. Select pain score 0–10.
5. Add a comment if needed.
6. Click:

```text
E përfundova
```

## Pain score rule

Use this scale:

```text
0 = no pain
1–3 = mild pain
4–6 = moderate pain
7–10 = strong pain / stop
```

If your pain is 7/10 or higher:

```text
Stop the exercise and contact your physiotherapist.
```

## AI Movement Check

Some exercises may show:

```text
AI Movement Check
```

This uses your phone camera to give feedback about movement quality.

AI can help with:

- body visibility
- movement control
- symmetry
- alignment
- stability

AI cannot:

- diagnose you
- prescribe therapy
- replace your physiotherapist
- decide if an exercise is medically safe for you

## Camera safety

Before using AI Movement Check:

- place the phone safely
- make sure your full body is visible if needed
- use good light
- do not rush
- stop if you feel pain, dizziness or insecurity

Video is not stored in the MVP. The app stores only score, feedback and alert type.

## When to contact physiotherapist

Contact your physiotherapist if:

- pain is 7/10 or higher
- pain increases after every session
- you feel numbness or tingling
- you feel weakness
- you feel dizzy
- you are not sure how to do the exercise
- AI score is low and you cannot correct the movement

## What not to do

Do not:

- share your code
- do exercises from another patient’s plan
- continue with strong pain
- use AI as diagnosis
- change your therapy without physiotherapist advice

## Simple patient message

Clinic can send this message to a patient:

```text
Përshëndetje. Ky është kodi juaj personal për Fizioterapia ime: [KODI].
Hyni te Patient Portal ose skanoni QR code. Kryeni ushtrimet sipas planit dhe raportoni dhimbjen 0–10 pas ushtrimit.
Nëse dhimbja është 7/10 ose më shumë, ndaloni ushtrimin dhe na kontaktoni.
AI Movement Check është vetëm feedback për lëvizje dhe nuk zëvendëson fizioterapeutin.
```
